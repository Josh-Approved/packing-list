# Privacy

Your data stays with you.

Your trips, items, packers, and packed state live in a database on your phone — that's the only copy the app keeps. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no tracking, and no analytics — the app doesn't profile you or send your lists anywhere. There's no server of mine that holds your data, and no way for me to see it.

## Where your data can go

- **On this device.** Everything stays in app storage on your phone.
- **In your device's iCloud backup, if you have it on.** Like any app, Packing List's local data is included in the whole-device backup Apple makes for you automatically. That backup is yours, on your own iCloud account — I don't operate it or see it. (You can exclude the app in iOS Settings → iCloud if you'd rather it not be backed up.)
- **In a file you export yourself.** If you tap Export, the app hands a single file to the system share sheet and you choose where it goes. Nothing leaves the app until you pick a destination.

## What's collected

Nothing. No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party sharing.
