# Privacy

Your data stays with you.

Your trips, items, packers, and packed state live in a database on your phone. That is the only copy this app creates. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no tracking, and no analytics, and the app doesn't profile you.

## Sharing a trip

When you share a trip with someone, the two phones keep each other up to date by passing small encrypted messages back and forth. A few things are true about this, by design:

- **No account, ever.** Sharing is a link or a QR code. Nobody signs up for anything.
- **We don't run a server.** The messages travel through free, public infrastructure that we don't own, operate, or pay for.
- **Nobody in the middle can read it.** Each message is locked (end-to-end encrypted) so that only the phones on your shared trip can open it. The infrastructure it passes through only ever sees scrambled text and a random label, never your packing list, and never anything about you.
- **The list lives on the phones, not out there.** That infrastructure is a courier, not a filing cabinet. Your trip is always held on each device; the messages are just how the phones stay in step.
- **Only trips you choose.** A trip is only ever shared if you share it, and only with the people you send the link or QR code to.

## Where your data can go

- **On this device.** Everything stays in app storage on your phone.
- **In your own iCloud or Android backup, if you have it on.** Like any app, this app's data is included in the device backup your platform makes for you. That backup is yours, on your own account, which you control. We don't operate it and can't see it.
- **To someone you share a trip with.** Only trips you explicitly share, as described above.
- **In a file you export yourself.** If you tap Export, the app hands a single file to the system share sheet and you choose where it goes. Nothing leaves the app until you pick a destination.

## What's collected

Nothing. We collect nothing, we hold nothing, and there is no telemetry of any kind. Nothing is ever sold or shared with anyone else.
