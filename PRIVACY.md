# Privacy

Your data stays with you.

Your trips, items, packers, and packed state live in a database on your phone. By default that's the only copy. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no tracking, and no analytics — the app doesn't profile you or send your lists anywhere. There's no server of mine that holds your data, and no way for me to see it.

## Where your data can go

- **On this device.** By default everything stays in app storage on your phone.
- **In your own iCloud, if you turn on backup.** Tapping "Back up to iCloud" in Settings keeps a private copy of your trips in your personal iCloud, so they survive a lost or replaced phone. It lives in your own iCloud account, which you control — I don't operate it and can't see it. (Apple devices only, and it stays off until you turn it on.)
- **In your device's iCloud backup, if you have it on.** Like any app, Packing List's local data is also included in the whole-device backup Apple makes for you. That backup is yours, on your iCloud account — I don't operate it or see it.
- **In a file you export yourself.** If you tap Export, the app hands a single file to the system share sheet and you choose where it goes. Nothing leaves the app until you pick a destination.

## What's collected

Nothing. No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party sharing. The iCloud copy above is your own — it isn't collected by me.
