# Privacy

Packing List keeps your data on your device.

Your trips, items, packers, and packed state are stored in a local database on the phone itself. That's the only copy this app creates.

No servers. No copies of your lists. No access to them. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics — the app doesn't track what you do or send anything anywhere.

## Where your data can go

- **On this device.** Everything lives in app storage on your phone.
- **In your own iCloud backup, if you have it on.** Like any app, Packing List's data is included in the device backup Apple makes for you. That backup is yours — it runs on your iCloud account, which you control. I don't operate it and can't see it.
- **In a file you export yourself.** If you tap Export, the app hands a single file to the system share sheet and you choose where it goes. Nothing is shared until you pick a destination.

## What's collected

Nothing. There is no data collection, no third-party sharing, and no telemetry of any kind.
